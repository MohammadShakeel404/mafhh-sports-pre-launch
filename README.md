# Pre-Launch Landing Page

## What It Is
The pre-launch landing page is a marketing tool designed to capture the interest of potential users before a product officially launches. This page serves as a way to inform the audience about the upcoming product and build anticipation.

## How It Works
When users visit the pre-launch landing page, they are presented with information about the upcoming product. They can often sign up for notifications or provide their email addresses to receive updates about the launch. The landing page typically includes engaging visuals and a call-to-action to encourage sign-ups.

## Features
- **Countdown Timer:** Displays the time remaining until the official launch.
- **Email Sign-Up Form:** Captures user emails to keep them updated about the launch.
- **Social Sharing Options:** Integrates social media buttons for users to share information about the product.
- **Visual Content:** Engaging images or videos that highlight the product's value.

## Why Pre-Launch Pages Are Important
Pre-launch pages are crucial for generating buzz and building an audience before a product launches. They help in:
- **Creating Anticipation:** Customers feel involved and excited about the upcoming product.
- **Gathering Feedback:** Early interaction with potential users can provide valuable insights.
- **Building an Email List:** An email list can be one of the most effective marketing channels for future campaigns.
